# ValSpams for TBC Anniversary

ValSpams is a (hopefully) lightweight announcer addon in the spirit of the classic WakeSpams addon (credit @Cyrila-BloodfeatherEU) for TBC Anniversary.

The goal is clean announcements for offensives, defensives, interrupts, taunts, utility spells and CC. There are some options baked in to adjust how those announcements appear, and a toggle for almost every imaginable announcement.

## Install

I highly recommend downloading from CurseForge, and updating as usual with CurseForge once a week or two.

Alternatively, download it from the CurseForge website, extract the ValSpams folder, and place it into your /_anniversary_/interface/addons directory.

## Use

Use `/vs` or `/valspams` to open the options panel.

## Current Features

- Offensives and defensives with target whisper where applicable/logical.
- Taunt announcements; paladin taunt might need cleaning up.
- Most major CC spells with cast and breakage.
- Interrupts.
- Important tank outcome tracking for selected paladin, warrior, and druid spells.
  - e.g. judgement, exorcism, devastate, shield slam, mangle.
- Dispels and purge-style effects.
  - potentially spammy in raids or any situation with obnoxious dispelling.
- Trinket activation tracking.
  - turned off by default, available in options.
